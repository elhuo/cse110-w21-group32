Spl/ice Pomodoro is a Pomodoro Timer catered to students. Work with the timer and prevent yourself from freezing up when studying and working!

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The creation of Francesco Cirillo, the Pomodoro Technique increases work productivity by dividing work into "pomodoros" and encouraging frequent breaks.

How To Properly Pomodoro

  • Decide on the task to be done.
  • Set the pomodoro timer (default 25 minutes).
  • Work on the task until the timer goes off.
    • Be sure to remove any distractions while you work!
    • If you finish early, feel free to use the time to plan or review your work.
  • Stop work when the timer rings and make a note of your progress.
  • Follow up every pomodoro with a short break and every four pomodoro with a long break.
