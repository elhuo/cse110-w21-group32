Spl/ice Pomodoro is a pomodoro timer catered for students. Work with the timer and prevent yourself from freezing up when studying and working!

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique increases work productivity by dividing work into "pomodoros", units comprised of a 25 minutes of work and 5 minutes of break. After 4 "pomodoros", you get a longer 30 minute break.

How To Properly Pomodoro

When the "pomo" tab is active, do everything in your power to remove all distractions and focus on your work. You'll find that most distractions can be put off until a break time. If you have to break focus and do something else, press the "stop" button. The pomodoro will not be completed.

After 25 minutes of work, stop what you're doing. Don't try to finish something up, your time of work is done. Take a break. You deserve it. Take your mind off of your work. Scroll through your phone, walk around. This break time is essential for staying focused during each "pomo"
